Hello! I'm Aida ("eye-duh")
I work at the intersection of exoplanets and galactic archaeology

I use large-scale surveys (e.g., Gaia, SDSS-V), data-driven tools, and spectroscopic/radial velocity observations to measure the properties (e.g., chemical abundances) of stars and their planetary systems

I think about:
(1) How much chemical variation exists within star forming regions? Between "doppelgänger" stars?
(2) How does planet formation imprint upon host star chemistry?
(3) Which planet formation processes dominate on large (galactic?!) scales?
